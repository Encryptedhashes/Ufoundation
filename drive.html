Mission Statement

You Foundations is Non-governmental organisation dedicated to providing support, resources and opportunities to marginalized communities. We strive to break the cycle of poverty, promote education, and foster personal growth, while empowering individuals to become agents of positive change to the country and beyond.

Vision Statement

You Foundation envisions a world where every youth and child thrives, empowered through education, mentorship, and community engagement, ultimately fostering a generation of resilient leaders who transform their communities

Objectives

  • To improve access to education for underprivileged children and youth
  • Empower women and girls through education, economic opportunities and leadership development
  • Promote social justice and human right, advocating for marginalized communities
  • Provide help and concealing to the helpless
  • To build a strong youth to serve our nation
  • To put smiles on the faces of humanity
  • To improve upon the lives of its members
  • Cultivate partnership with local organisations, governments, corporations for collaborative impact

Target Audience

  • Unprivileged children and youth
  • Low incomee families
  • Marginalized communities
  • Women and girls
  • Individuals and disabilities
